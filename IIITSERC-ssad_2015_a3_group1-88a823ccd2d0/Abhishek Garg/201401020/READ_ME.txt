This is donkey kong game written without using pygame.

CONTROLS:
w-MOVE UP
a-MOVE LEFT
d-MOVE RIGHT
s-MOVE DOWN
space-JUMP
q-QUIT

JUMP:
in a jump, the player goes one step above and then comes down at the same position.

A GREAT IDEA:
When in doubt, just try a jump. It can save you on ladders also.

GOING FROM LEVEL 1 TO LEVEL 2:
The queen is captured in a cage and player needs to capture the key(K) to free the queen. 

GOING FROM LEVEL 2 TO LEVEL 3:
The number of donkeys in increased from one to two.
